Quality Instruments

BHN SURGICALS PVT LTD is a manufacturer and supplier of quality Instruments for General Surgery, Obs&Gynec, Cardio-Thoracic / Vascular, Urology, Gastro-Entrology, Ortho, Neuro/Spine, ENT, Plastic Surgery and Hands Free Retractor Systems.

High Degree of precision adhering

BHN,s products are manufactured with high degree of precision adhering to Standard Operating Proceedures ( SOP’s ) in sophisticated machines of fine technology by skilled craftsmen under the able supervison of highly experienced production Managers monitoring quality control at every level of production.
